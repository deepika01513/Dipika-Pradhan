Welcome

Dr. Dipika Digdarshini Pradhan is a dedicated researcher and educator in the field of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering. She received her Ph.D. in Electronics from Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology (SVNIT), Surat, with a specialization in Experimental Analysis of Erbium doped Fiber Amplifier and Design optimization of EDFA, Raman and Hybrid amplifier for DWDM system. Her research focuses on fiber optics communication, nanomaterials, photonic biosensors, and wearable sensors, contributing significantly to the advancement of optical communication technologies.
